Our Story

Where every
loaf has a name

Rooted in Portuguese baking tradition and shaped by classic American flavors, Maple St Bakery has been a cornerstone of Naugatuck since the very beginning. Every morning before sunrise, our bakers fill the kitchen with the scent of warm dough, toasted sugar, and fresh pastry.

We believe good bread doesn't need shortcuts. What you taste is craft — passed down, perfected, and baked fresh daily.
